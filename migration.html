Skilled Visas

Australia has a strong economy because its migration policy ensures that it attracts the best and brightest people in the world. We want the right people with the right skills to do jobs in areas where Australia has a skills shortage.

If you have an occupation that Australia needs and meet the points test then you may be able to receive a government nomination for permanent residency to live and work in Australia.

These skilled visas (for example subclass 189 & 190) are the most common way people migrate to Australia permanently. You do not need an employer to sponsor you and you can include partners and other family members to come with you.

Partner Visas

Being able to choose your partner is a fundamental human right. There are a range of partner visas available to let partners (including de facto) of Australian citizens or Permanent Residents to come and stay in Australia together.

Unfortunately partner visas have one of the highest refusal rates in Australia. This is often because couples are unaware of how to best demonstrate that their relationship is genuine and ongoing..

Let us help you with presenting strong evidence of your spouse relationship so that you can start building a life and family together here in Australia.

Business Visas

There are a range of business-related visas available in Australia. For example, the Business Innovation and Investment visa allow people with strong business skills to come to Australia to operate a new or existing business. You need to be nominated for this visa, have an ownership stake in a business and meet a points test.


This visa allows you to stay in Australia for 4 years and provides a pathway to permanent residency if you meet certain requirements. Speak to us to help guide you towards the right business visa for you.
