About Us


LEVISA – Education | Visas

We provide expert advice on education and visa matters to help you come to Australia to visit, study, work or stay permanently. Our highly qualified education counsellors and registered migration agents can give you honest, professional and trustworthy advice to empower you to make the right decisions about your future. We are partners with some of the top educational institutions in Australia and together we can help you apply for the right courses and qualifications that will lead to a successful job and career. We are also migration experts, so we can assist with obtaining skilled migration visas, partner visas, work visas and others.

About us

LEVISA has over 10 year history of helping our local community, we are proud of this and it’s something we’ll always continue to do. LEVISA was started by a pair of Vietnamese-Australian professionals living in Canberra who were an international student and the son of a refugee. Back then Vietnamese international students and newly arrived migrants came to us looking for help with filling out forms and applications for housing, utilities, schooling, social services and visa related paperwork. While most of us may take such things for granted – a little help from us made a big difference in the lives of these people. The modest support we able to offer help set them on the path towards become valuable members of Australian society. Over time we built trust and confidence within the community. We continued to work with the international student associations and Institutions to provide education counselling and migration assistance. In 2018, LEVISA Pty Ltd was formed to share Canberra’s educational services and offerings with new and emerging markets in Asia, including regional provinces in Vietnam. We plan to continue our story and grow to offer education and visa services all over the world.

Why choose us?

At LEVISA we have been in your shoes. We were international students once too. We’ve been through it all so we help you to avoid the pitfalls so that you can experience the very best that Australia has to offer. We will be with you all the way. LEVISA staff have over 10 years of experience in international student education, government and migration law. We have certified professional education counsellors and registered migration agents. We offer a personalised service and will work with you to develop a study plan and package that will suit your needs. Helping you come to Australia is just the beginning of our job – we want to share with you all the tips, knowledge and hints to help you settle in and enjoy Australia. Whether it’s about how to apply for a tax file number, finding a part time job or best places to enjoy an Aussie meat pie and beer – we’ll have you covered. This is all just part of our service and promise that we’ll look after you. We also speak your language, we are fluent in English and Vietnamese.
