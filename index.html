Migration Services

Whether you want to come to Australia for study, business, family, leisure or any one of other reasons, we can get you there.

Student Visa

We have helped many students to undertake their dream course and embark on their own path towards a successful and rewarding career.

Skilled Visa

We specialise in skilled visas (189 & 190) and the Canberra Matrix process. We help find out your eligibility requirements for your nomination place.

Partner Visa

We help present a strong case for your spouse relationship so that you can start building a life and family together here in Australia.

Parent Visa

We help you determine if your parent or guardian is eligible for a parent visa and which type of parent visa is best suited for your family’s circumstances.

Business Visa

There are a range of business-related visas available in Australia. Speak to us to help guide you towards the right business visa for you.

Citizenship

We can help determine if you are eligible for citizenship and apply for you. We arrange interviews, citizenship tests and ceremonies.

Our Story

Levisa started from humble beginnings - we had a simple goal of helping our family, friends and community with advice on legal issues, educational options ,government forms and settling in Australia.

While our business and client base has grown over time - our core principles remain the same. Trust, confidence and integrity. We strive to look after all our clients as if you were our family and friends. This is what sets us apart. Our business model is built on the support of the community and we take our responsibilities in acting in the best interests of our clients to heart. Our advice is based on our genuine experience - we know what it is like to be an international student, a hardworking migrant and a proud Australian. You will always be in good hands with Levisa.
