Education

Education Service

At LEVISA we specialise in helping people to come to Australia to study. We can help you find the perfect course to study at one of the best high schools, TAFEs or universities in Australia – the one that is right for you. We can organise your student visa and take care of all the paperwork. Let us worry about all the small things so that you can focus on the big things in your life – like learning, experiencing everything Australia has to offer and building your own future. We have helped many students from Vietnam, Malaysia, Singapore and the Philippines to come to Australia to realise their study dreams. We can help you too. Let us lead the way – we have expert education counsellors and registered migration agents that can create study plan that is tailored just for you. Everyone is different and we all have our own stories. We want to hear yours so we can help you write the next chapter in Australia.


How it works

Step 1: We listen to you and show you the best options

– our education counsellor will chat with you about your study background, professional interests and career goals. They know all about what’s on offer and will give a list of course options to help you find the one that will set you on the right path.

Step 2: Choose your education institution and city

– we will advise you which TAFE, university or educational institution offers your preferred course and discuss which Australian city you want live and study in. We’ll assess whether you meet the study requirements and let you know about all the costs and benefits involved.

Step 3: We check whether you can get a visa

– our registered migration agent will check whether you meet the eligibility for you to obtain a student visa and what documentation you will need to provide.

Step 4: We look after all of the paperwork for you

– this includes talking to your education institution, organising your letter of offer and arranging your enrolment as well as lodging your visa application. All you need to do is to sit back and wait for your visa outcome.

Step 5: We welcome you and make you feel at home

– Collect Visa Grant Letter and arrive to Australia. We have free mentoring lessons and workshops to help you get settled in Australia and ready to embark on the next chapter of your life.

Student visas

Australia welcomes genuine international students here to enrich our social, cultural and intellectual community.

However, student visas are also the number one most refused visa in Australia. Sadly, many students do not know how to prove that they are a genuine temporary stay international student.

Don’t miss out on your education future, let us help you present a strong case as to why you should be given the opportunity to study in Australia. We have helped many students to undertake their dream course and embark on their own path towards a successful and rewarding career.

We can help you study with reputable Australian education institutions at different education levels such as:

  • High-School
  • Diploma
  • Undergrad and Post-grad
